About Us

At AfrOganiks, we strongly believe that NATURE has all answers to all health problems. All human health conditions have a solution, except if the plant has not yet been identified and that is why we are totally committed to researching on plants and inventing evidence-based formulations that help individuals live better, healthier and happier.

Our efficacious products are widely accepted and are backed up with clinical studies.

Our journey dates back to 2002, when our Co-Founder, Dr. Jeph Akaehie who was living in a small village in Owerri West, Imo State, Nigeria with his late father ( PLANT RESEARCHER), picked deeper interest in medicinal plants after he was healed of hernia using herbs.

In early 2002, there were lots of youths infected with various STD’s in the small village where Dr. Jeph lived and due to his closeness to his late father, the youths trusted and confided in him for treatments.

He decided to try out some of the medicinal plants he was taught and boom, more and more people were coming including expectant couples and the amazing outcomes announced us.

Today, AfrOganiks is a leading manufacturer of quality organic dietary and nutritional supplements geared towards helping millions of Africans achieve total wellness
